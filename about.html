OUR STORY

From small fixes
to full renovations.

Universe Construction was founded by a tradesperson, not a businessperson — which shows in how every job gets done. We started small, taking on repairs and odd jobs and doing them properly, and grew by referral rather than advertising.

Today we handle renovations, repairs, painting and roof maintenance side by side, but the standard hasn't moved: every job gets the same attention to detail as the first one.
